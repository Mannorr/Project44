Why It Matters

Your giving goes all the way.

Every contribution to PROJECT44 — whether financial or in-kind — reaches the children we serve. No fluff. No overhead waste. Just real resources reaching real children in Abuja and Lagos, Nigeria. We are transparent, passionate, and accountable. When you partner with us, you will see the impact.
