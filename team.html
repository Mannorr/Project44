· Meet the Core ·

We are PROJECT44.

A group of young people who refused to wait for someone else to show up. Meet the core — the hands, hearts, and minds behind every outreach.

Ene Deborah Ohemu

Founder

Ene is a medical student at Babcock University, a lover of Jesus, and an all-round creative. She founded PROJECT44 with a simple but powerful belief — that hope in the underprivileged younger generation needed to be born. Through the provision of basic amenities and the message of the Gospel, her goal is to offer every child a glimpse of a better life, both in this world and the one to come.

Femi-Olagundoye Mojoyinoluwa

Head of Operations, Lagos

Olamide is a medical student at Babcock University with a vision for a world where every person — regardless of social class, gender, race, or age — has access to the basic needs of life. She joined PROJECT44 because she believes the most important gift anyone can receive is the gift of salvation. She brings that conviction to every outreach she leads.

Ochanya E. Agbo-Ojobo

Head of Operations, Abuja

Ochanya is a digital artist and language enthusiast who brings both creativity and conviction to her work at PROJECT44. Her motivation is deeply spiritual — she is here for the expansion of God's kingdom here on Earth. That purpose shapes how she leads, serves, and shows up for every child in Abuja.

Adeniran Oluwanifemi

Head of Media Operations

Nifemi is a visual and brand designer who works with both corporate organisations and individuals to tell brand stories. At PROJECT44, he ensures that the foundation's story is told consistently and authentically across every platform and in every community they reach. Because the story matters as much as the work.
