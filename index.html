PROJECT44 Foundation

Every Child Deserves to Be Loved, Seen, and Given Hope.

A faith-driven foundation reaching the most vulnerable children with physical care, spiritual truth, and the promise of a better future.

Partner With Us Learn More
"To be the hands and heart of Christ to children in need — demonstrating God's love through reliable, compassionate, and community-driven humanitarian action centred on the message of the Gospel."
What We Do

Caring for the Whole Child.

Each PROJECT44 outreach is designed to reach body, mind, and soul — because real transformation means addressing every part of a child's life.

We Nourish

From food and clothing to medical supplies and hygiene essentials, we show up with what children need to survive and thrive.

We Educate

We equip children with learning materials, books, and educational resources that open doors to a future full of possibility.

We Transform

Beyond physical relief, we share the message of the Gospel — offering children the most powerful gift: the hope of eternal life.

Our Vision

A World Where Every Child Knows They Are Loved.

We envision a world where every child, regardless of background, grows up with the assurance that they are loved by God, supported by community, and destined for a life of purpose and hope.

Who We Reach

The Children the World Too Often Overlooks.

PROJECT44 directs its work toward the most vulnerable members of the younger generation — children living with disabilities, orphans, and those with special needs including cerebral palsy and autism. These are children who deserve not just relief, but dignity, love, and a future.

Why It Matters

Change That Lasts a Lifetime.

Temporary relief is not enough. We are committed to holistic support that addresses both the physical and spiritual needs of every child we reach — because true transformation outlasts a single visit.
